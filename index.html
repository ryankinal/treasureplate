We went down to Avon's Bluebrick Recordings for a few days and started recording our first album: Engine Core: Phenomenal.

With our good friend Jesse Sprinkle at the helm, it was an easy, smooth process. We even had a huge group of our friends in the studio to lend a hand.

Now that the first mixes are coming in, we're SUPER EXCITED to share what we ended up with, so keep an eye out for some sneaky releses, and maybe even a proper music video!

Skyler Smith interviewed three of our four crew members after a show at the California Brew Haus. We talk inspiration, influences, and instruments.

You guys have a pretty extensive live show going on. From costumes, to spoken lines, what would you say is the biggest inspiration on all that?

Ryan: Were nerds!

Brian: Yeah, we're nerdy! But growing up I really liked music, I was really into theatre, and I found myself like "this is awesome, but it's not super satisfying". So, I asked myself "what can I do to kind of bring all these ideas together?"" and so I got together with a few people and it was this. It was a "Theatre Thing".

Josh: And it's something that's totally different, which is fantastic.
